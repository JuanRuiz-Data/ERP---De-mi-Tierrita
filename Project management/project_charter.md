# Project Charter – *ERP for De mi Tierrita*

## General Information

- **Project Name:** ***ERP – De mi Tierrita***  
- **Project Description:** Custom Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) System for E-commerce Management  
- **Project Start Date:** January, 2026
- **Estimated Completion Date:** June, 2026  
- **Project Manager:** Juan Ruiz  
- **Version:** 1.0  

---

## Project Purpose

The purpose of this project is to design, develop, and implement a **custom ERP system** to support the operational, administrative, and strategic management of the **De mi Tierrita** e-commerce business.

This ERP will centralize processes related to products, inventory, sales, logistics, finance, and customer management, while also serving as an **educational and experimental platform** developed as part of the workshop *“Building an e-commerce from scratch.”*

---

## Project Objectives

- Design a modular and scalable ERP architecture tailored to the needs of a growing e-commerce business.  
- Centralize key business processes such as inventory, orders, suppliers, customers, and finances.  
- Integrate the ERP with the Shopify e-commerce platform.  
- Automate operational workflows to reduce manual processes and errors.  
- Serve as a hands-on learning case study combining software engineering, data engineering, mathematics, and project management.

---

## Project Scope

### Included

- ERP architecture design and documentation.  
- Development of core ERP modules:
  - Product and inventory management  
  - Orders and sales tracking  
  - Suppliers and logistics  
  - Customers and customer service  
  - Accounting and financial records  
- Integration with Shopify and external services.  
- Role-based access and permission management.  
- Basic reporting and operational visibility.  
- Technical documentation and learning materials for workshop participants.

### Excluded

- Advanced business intelligence dashboards.  
- Machine learning or predictive analytics.  
- Full internationalization and multi-currency support.  
- Third-party ERP replacement or migration.

## Success Criteria

- Fully functional ERP supporting daily operations of the De mi Tierrita e-commerce.  
- Seamless integration with Shopify for products, orders, and inventory.  
- Modular architecture allowing future extensions and scalability.  
- Reduction of manual operational tasks.  
- Clear and complete documentation usable for both business operations and educational purposes.

---

## Initial Risks Identified

- Scope creep due to evolving business requirements.  
- Integration challenges with external platforms (Shopify APIs).  
- Limited adoption due to learning curve.  
- Balancing production needs with educational objectives.
